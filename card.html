Giant Panda

The giant panda does not hibernate but will shelter in caves or hollow trees in very cold weather.

  • Scientific Name: Ailuropoda melanoleuca
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Average Height: 60-90 centimeters
  • Habitat: Remote, mountainous regions of central China

It is easily recognized by the large, distinctive black patches around its eyes, over the ears, and across its round body. A panda's daily diet consists almost entirely of the leaves, stems and shoots of various bamboo species.
